About Me

Hi,

I Code. I Sketch.
I aspire to combine the two to create experiences that celebrate human expression.

An individual driven by visual cues, whose thoughts gallop amidst a loud constant chatter, I imprint an image of the things I see, am enticed to write down everything I hear and obsess about what I need to do next and how I am going to get there.

Being a developer by education, a designer by passion, and an aesthete by heart, I am constantly striving to explore the nexus between art & technology. If you share the same interests, we should definetly chat.

What I do

I am currently consulting as a Unity Developer for a Project at Building 8, Facebook. Prior to this I was a VR software developer at YBVR where I worked on the client side of the application, built primarily using Unity 3D.

Rapid Prototyping, candid brainstorming, and engaging interactions are what drives me to work. I recently graduated from USC with a masters in Computer Graphics. My coursework was primarily focused on applied signal processing, computer graphics, physics-based simulation and VR.

My core focus includes :

  • Computer Graphics
  • Virtual Reality /Augmented Reality /Mixed Reality
  • Physics-based simulation
  • Rapid Prototyping
  • Unity 3D
  • C++
