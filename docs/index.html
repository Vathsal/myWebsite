About Me

Hi! Bringing together innovative technologies, open standards and creative processes, I am a developer who aspires to create interactive experiences which are engaging and intellectually stimulating. An individual driven by visual cues, whose thoughts gallop amidst a loud constant chatter, I imprint an image of the things I see, am enticed to write down everything I hear and obsess about what I need to do next and how I am going to get there.

An ardent admirer of minimalistic design and creative detailing, I also like to explore the myriad ways through which one can impact life with design. My varying interests and pursuits have helped me stay curious and open-minded and I strive to be a designer by passion, developer by profession, an esthete by vocation and a conversationalist by character. At the heart of all of these lies a young man who appreciates life and its simplicity. The iconic Julia Childs once said “People who love to eat are always the best people” and I absolutely swear by it. Good food and soulful music is all it takes to make me a happy man!
